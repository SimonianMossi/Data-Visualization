My Favourite Data Visualization

After thoroughly searching the web for Data Visualizations, I can say with certainty that my favourite style of Data Visualization is Scrollytelling, and from all of those I encountered on my search, the one that stood out the most was Color Meaning

First, it was the theme of the Visualization that picked my interest, but after scrolling through the whole thing, I was mesmerized by the colors and the animated transitions.

I was very impressed about how much fun I had while reading it and how eager I was to absorb the information that was about to come.

But what impressed me the most about this Visualization is the fact that I still remember the things I read even though I went through this Scrollytelling only once!
