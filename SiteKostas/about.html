  • Baby steps

    Back in primary school, I imagined I would be making video games one day, and I would draw extensive drafts of silly game ideas on paper.

  • First games

    In high school I discovered a few game engines (RPG Maker 2k and Game Maker 5.0) and made my first attempts at game development, making simple mini-games and slowly getting better at it.

  • Air Force Academy, programming, and more games

    Got in the Air Force Academy and graduated as an aircraft electronics engineer. My dissertation was a material management platform involving a Web Server, Android and QR codes. In the meantime, had some chances to learn new technologies (Java, PHP, SQL) and took up Unity as a preferred game engine. Worked on various solo and team projects afterwards.

  • Army Programmers School

    Enrolled in the Army School of Software Development and Analysis and graduated top of class. My dissertation was an education management system for the Air Force Academy, written in Java EE.

  • Postgraduate Studies

    Enrolled in the Computer Science postgraduate program of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.
