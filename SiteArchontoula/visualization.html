VISUALIZATION

The vizualization below tackles the issue of covid 19 as many have other studies since the year 2020.
What I found particularly interesting is it's focus on the often overlooked casualties of this
global pandemic that have survived the virus but have been pushed into poverty from the early 2019 to the end of 2020.
I find that the design is simple enough so as to not overwhelm the eye but very clearly conveys the message it is supposed to.
The three different poverty tiers are easily distinguishable with the use of different colors
and the bottom panel presenting the statistics for each country is also easy to interpret. Overall I believe that
it is important to draw focus on the aftermath of the continuous lockdowns in order for each state to take action
for its people. With this in mind, if I were to make any changes in the visulization, I would like to also be able to
view the absolute numbers accompanied by the percentages.
